About Me

A hands-on technical leader, keeping my “head in the clouds,” I have a keen interest in both public and private cloud-based computing technologies. More specifically, these relate to event streaming microservice architectures running within container orchestration platforms utilizing technologies such as the Go and Java programming languages, Kafka, SpringBoot, Docker, Kubernetes, Helm, Linkerd, et al.

With over 20 years of system development experience in varying languages and environments, I have great breadth of experience with both small, specialized applications to large, service-oriented architectures. These include development methodologies, as well as functions within the entire system development lifecycle (SDLC).

Highly adaptive to changing situations and needs with a strong belief in using the right technology for the right project. Excellent communication skills, working well within a team as well as individually.
